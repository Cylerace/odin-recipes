Fried Rice


Ingredients

  • Cooked, chilled rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots, onions, green onions, peas
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Toasted sesame oil
  • Olive Oil

Steps and Instructions

  1. Scramble eggs using olive oil and transfer eggs to a separate plate
  2. Saut√© veggies and garlic until soft and cooked through
  3. Add the chilled rice to pan. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, and oyster sauce. Combine with veggies and stir for 3 minutes until rice and veggies brown
  4. Add green onions, sesame oil, and scrambled eggs and stir
  5. Remove pan from heat and season with salt and pepper
  6. Add other sauces if needed and serve
