Pico de Gallo

Ingredients

  • 3 Roma Tomatoes
  • 1/2 White Onion
  • 2 Medium Jalapeño or Serrano Peppers
  • 1 lime
  • Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Finely Chopped Fresh Cilantro

Steps and Instructions

  1. Chop the onions, jalapeño, cilantro, lime, and combine into a bowl
  2. Add salt and allow it to marinate while you chop the tomatoes and cilantro
  3. Combine chopped tomatoes and cilantro with everything else and mix
  4. Let it rest of 15 minutes
  5. Serve
